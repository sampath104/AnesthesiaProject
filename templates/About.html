About Our Project

The Anesthesia Complication Predictor leverages advanced Machine Learning algorithms to provide accurate risk assessment for anesthesia procedures. Our goal is to empower healthcare providers with AI-powered tools that enhance patient safety through early risk identification and preventive care.

This system employs Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost models trained on comprehensive patient data, achieving remarkable prediction accuracy while ensuring fast and intuitive risk assessment for anesthesiologists and surgical teams.

By analyzing patient demographics, medical history, surgery details, and clinical notes using advanced preprocessing techniques including TF-IDF vectorization and feature engineering, our system provides actionable insights for safer anesthesia management.

Our Mission

To utilize Artificial Intelligence and machine learning to provide accurate, rapid, and accessible anesthesia risk assessment, improving patient safety and surgical outcomes in healthcare facilities worldwide.

Our Vision

A future where advanced AI technology supports anesthesiologists in every operating room, enabling data-driven decision-making, reducing complications, and ensuring the highest standards of patient care during surgical procedures.
